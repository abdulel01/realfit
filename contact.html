Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book a party?

We recommend booking at least 3-4 weeks in advance to secure your preferred date and time, especially for weekend parties. Popular slots fill up quickly!

What's included in the party packages?

All our party packages include dedicated party hosts, access to amazing play areas/activities, delicious hot food for the kids, drinks, and awesome goody bags to take home. Specific activities vary by package. Check our Party Packages page for full details on each option.

Is there a minimum or maximum number of children?

Yes, minimum numbers apply and vary by package (usually 10-15 children). The maximum number of guests is typically 30, but please check the specific package details. The birthday child is included in the guest count.

Do you cater for dietary requirements?

Absolutely! We can cater for various dietary requirements, including vegetarian, gluten-free, and common allergies. Please inform us of any specific needs when making your booking so we can make the necessary arrangements.

Can parents stay during the party?

Parents are welcome to stay! We have comfortable seating areas near the party zones. For Go-Kart parties, parental supervision might be required for younger racers.

What are the age ranges for the parties?

Our parties are generally designed for children aged 4-12. Some packages, like Go-Karting or Climbing, have specific minimum age requirements (e.g., 6+ or 5+). Please see the Party Packages page for details.
